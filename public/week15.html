Erasmus week 14 - day 1

Today was a very exciting day because this week would be our long waited Lapland trip. We had to wake up early and travel to the trainstation. There we stepped on the bus and we started our 25hour roadtrip to Lapland. We had a lot of fun on the bus and we played some games. We also watched some movies and we had a lot of fun. We also did some schoolwork and prepared for the next week.

Erasmus week 14 - day 2

Today we had some free time in lapland. We decided to go for a walk and discover the beautiful nature.

Erasmus week 14 - day 3

Today we went on a snowshoe walk at night. This was very cool and the shoes are very strange. They are very big and you have to walk very strange. It was fun because we needed to make our own path to the top of the mountain. It was a great experience and we had a lot of fun.

Erasmus week 14 - day 4

Today we went to the fjords of Norway. To get there we needed to travel 4 hours by bus. But it was worthed. The view was insane, and the nature was beautiful. We decided to dive into the cold water and then go into the sauna that was provided. It was a great experience and we had a lot of fun.

Erasmus week 14 - day 5

Today was the highlight of the week. We went on a husky ride. It was a great experience and we had a lot of fun. Meeting the dogs and riding the sled was a great experience. We also had a lot of fun with the dogs and we played some games with them. It was a great experience and we had a lot of fun.

Erasmus week 14 - day 6

Today was the last activity of the week. I went on a snowmobile ride. I was lucky because I was alone on a snowmobile. I had a lot of fun and I had a great experience. I also saw the northern lights. It was a great experience and I had a lot of fun.

Erasmus week 14 - day 7

Today we woke up very early to head back to Skövde. On our way back we stopped in Rovaniemie to visit the Santa Claus village. It was a great experience and we had a lot of fun. We also did some shopping and we bought some souvenirs. After that we continued our journey back to Skövde. It was a great experience and we had a lot of fun.
