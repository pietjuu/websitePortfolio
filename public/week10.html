Erasmus week 10 - day 1 and 2

The first 2 days are more or less chill days. We did not have anything planned and decided to join the local volleyball and basketball team. We had a lot of fun and we are planning to join them more often.

Erasmus week 10 - day 3

Today was a very important day. I had all my exams on this day. I was very nervous and I studied a lot. lucky for me everything was going well and I passed all my exams.

Erasmus week 10 - day 4

Because everybody was done with the exams we decided to go to the local small swimming pool. We had a lot of fun and we are planning to go more often. We did all the slides, played some games, went to the bubble bath and had a lot of fun.

Erasmus week 10 - day 5

Today we went costume shopping because halloween is close and we had a halloween party planned. We had a lot of fun and we are looking forward to the party. I dressed up as Spiderbar and Matthew dressed up as Partyman. It was a fun way to change the avengers characters.

Erasmus week 10 - day 6

Today was the day of the halloween party. We had a lot of fun and we are planning to do this more often. We had a lot of drinks, played some games and danced a lot. It was a fun night.

Erasmus week 10 - day 7

Today was a rest day. We were all very tired from the party and we decided to stay in bed all day.
