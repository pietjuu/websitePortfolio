Erasmus week 7 - week recap

This was one of the most relaxing weeks so far. We did not have any trips planned and we had a lot of time to catch up on schoolwork. We did go to the gym a few times and had some nice dinners with friends. We also went to the store to buy some meat and drinks for a BBQ in Billingen. We had a great time and the food was delicious. After the BBQ we went back home and watched a movie. We also did some schoolwork and prepared for the next week.
