Erasmus week 14 - day 1 and 2

the first 2 days were chill days, we went to the gym and had some nice dinners with friends. The cool thing was that we finally saw our first snow in Sweden. It was a pretty stunning view to wake up and see everything in the snow.

Erasmus week 14 - day 3

Today was highlighted with a trip to the ice hockey game. It was a great experience and we had a lot of fun. It is interesting to see that they fight a lot and it was very spectacular to see.

Erasmus week 14 - day 4

Today was a chill day, we went to the gym and did some homework. In the evening we did a party with some local friends. It was a great experience and we had a lot of fun.

Erasmus week 14 - day 5

Today we went to Göthenburg to watch a women football game. It was a championsleague matchup against real madrid. Today for us, the local team won with 2–1 after trailing 0–1. It was a great experience and we had a lot of fun.

Erasmus week 14 - day 6

Today we went back to a icehockey game. It was a great experience and we had a lot of fun.

Erasmus week 14 - day 7

To cap of the week we decided to party with some local friends. It was a great experience and we had a lot of fun.
