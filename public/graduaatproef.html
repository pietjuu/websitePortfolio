Thesis for Associate Degree in System and Network Administration.

A part of the second year of System and Network Administration involves carrying out a practical project at our internship site. I conducted my project at the company EFFIX, and it was focused on security, which is personally the most interesting aspect of IT for me. Due to my interest in this field, I plan to continue my studies next year in applied computer science, specializing in cybersecurity. Securing data and making it challenging for hackers is something I am eager to delve into.

The project at EFFIX revolved around security, a topic I have been interested in for a long time. My fascination began when I had the opportunity to set up a firewall for the first time. This experience revealed to me the vast amount of work in this sector of IT and the depth of this subject. I am eager to further explore this area, and this project has already provided me with significant insights.

I found this assignment to be very positive. Conducting a project independently has been a tremendous learning experience for me. I had to significantly expand my knowledge of security to successfully complete this project.

Working at EFFIX was always enjoyable. There was something new to do every day, contributing to my continuous learning. Every colleague was ready to assist us and present new challenges. I had the opportunity to experience various aspects of IT, such as working on the helpdesk, accompanying colleagues to set up networks at client locations, performing minor interventions independently, and handling small tasks in the office.
