Erasmus week 2 - day 1

Today, August 28, 2023, marked my first official school day. Having missed the introduction week due to exams back in Belgium, I had some catching up to do, starting with setting up my school account. Interestingly, unlike in Belgium, the office package isn't provided here. The setup was trickier than expected since everything was in Swedish, which we couldn't fully understand. However, this challenge turned out to be a blessing in disguise as it helped me meet new people who were also struggling with their accounts.

Once my account was set up, I joined some folks from Perenius, my dorm, and we headed to the student kick-off. The event was a bit chaotic since we were choosing individual subjects instead of enrolling in a complete course, which left us without a specific group. Despite this, we made the most of it, playing games like Mario Kart on the Wii and Just Dance on campus.

Later, we returned to our dorm to get ready for a big party night at KB, a popular student hangout. At the party, I met people from Xenia, another student dorm. I got to know Maud, Kim, and Niels from the Netherlands, who quickly became good friends. I also met Noah and David from Germany and Malta, respectively, and they too became close friends. That wraps up the day!
