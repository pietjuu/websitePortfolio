Erasmus week 8 - Day 1, 2, 3, 4

The first four days of this week are very quiet. We did not have any trips planned and we had a lot of time to catch up on schoolwork. We did go to the gym a few times and had some nice dinners with friends.

Erasmus week 8 - Day 5

Today I traveled to Göthenburg to prepare for the marathon that is tomorrow. Unfortunately, the train had a lot of delays and I arrived late. I had to hurry to the hotel to get some rest. I went to bed early to get some rest for the marathon.

Erasmus week 8 - Day 6

Today was the moment we have all been waiting for. The marathon in Göthenburg. I was very nervous and I woke up early to get some breakfast. I went to the starting point and I was ready to run. The marathon was very hard and I was very tired. I did not finish the marathon, but I am happy that I tried. I will try again next year. I did run 24 km.

Erasmus week 8 - Day 7

today was rest day. I was still very tired from the marathon, and I decided to stay in bed all day.
