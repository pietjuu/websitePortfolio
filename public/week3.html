Erasmus week 3 - day 1

Today, September 4, was a quiet day. After a week filled with parties, everyone was feeling pretty worn out. I spent the day playing basketball with the Spanish students and socializing with the students from Xenia. On the picture you can even see me dunk! It was a nice, chill day to recharge and enjoy some simple, fun activities.

Erasmus week 3 - day 2

Today was another laid-back day. I spent it relaxing in the common area with my fellow students, just unwinding and enjoying each other's company. Nothing major happened; we simply played card games and other games together. It was a nice, quiet day to just be together and have fun.

Erasmus week 3 - day 3

Today marked a slight change in my routine; finding sports activities was becoming challenging, so I decided to buy a gym subscription. This way, I can keep fit and stay active. Later, all the Erasmus students and I went bowling, which turned out to be a very fun experience! Noah and I teamed up, humorously naming our duo “Team Small PP.” We shared a lot of laughs, struck six strikes, and even made a playful challenge to do pushups whenever we missed a throw.

After bowling, I joined Maud, Niels, and some other students from Perenius to watch the first "Avengers" movie from Marvel. It was a great way to relax and unwind. We also took some time to plan our upcoming trip to Silverfallet! The day was full of good spirits and camaraderie, setting a positive tone for the upcoming adventures.
